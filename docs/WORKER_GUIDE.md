# BCF Worker Guide
**Ballycastle Climbing Frames — Worker Panel**

This guide covers everything you need to know to use the BCF Worker Panel from first login to completing a job.

---

## First Login — Setting Your Password

The first time you log in, you will need to set your own password.

1. BCF will give you a **temporary password**
2. Go to the Worker Panel login page
3. Enter your **email** and the **temporary password**
4. You'll be taken to a **Set Your Password** screen
5. Enter a new password (at least 8 characters)
6. Enter it again to confirm
7. Click **🔒 Set Password & Log In**
8. You're in — your jobs will load automatically

> **Keep your password safe.** If you forget it, contact BCF admin who can reset it for you.

---

## Logging In After Your First Visit

1. Go to the Worker Panel login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

---

## The Worker Panel — Overview

### On Your Phone (Mobile)

At the bottom of the screen you will see four tabs:

| Tab | What It Does |
|-----|-------------|
| 🏠 **Jobs** | See all your assigned jobs |
| 🔔 **Alerts** | See your notifications |
| 👤 **Profile** | Update your photo, name, and password |
| 🚪 **Sign Out** | Log out safely |

### On a Computer (Desktop)

- **Left sidebar:** Your profile, jobs list, and sign-out button
- **Right side:** The selected job detail

## Viewing Your Jobs

### On Mobile
1. Tap the **🏠 Jobs** tab at the bottom
2. You'll see a list of all jobs assigned to you
3. Each card shows:
   - Client name
   - Order number
   - Installation address
   - Installation date
   - Status (Pending / In Progress / Complete)
   - 🎂 if it's a birthday booking
4. Tap any job card to open it

### On Desktop
1. Your jobs are listed in the left sidebar
2. Click a job to open it on the right

## Working Through a Job

When you open a job you'll see two main sections:

- **Left — Build Stages:** Where you update your progress
- **Right — Client Details:** Client info, notes, and access instructions

---

## Build Stages — Step by Step

Build stages are the main steps of the installation (e.g., Order Confirmed, Groundwork, Frame Construction). **Complete them in order** — you cannot skip ahead.

---

### Step 1 — Start a Stage

1. Find the stage you're working on
2. Click the stage row to **expand** it
3. Click the **Start** button → the stage moves to 🔄 In Progress

---

### Step 2 — Add Tasks (if needed)

Tasks are the individual steps within a stage. Your manager may have already added tasks, but you can add your own.

1. Inside an expanded stage, click **+ Task**
2. Type what the task is (e.g., "Dig foundation trench")
3. Press **Enter** to save it

---

### Step 3 — Complete Tasks

As you finish each piece of work, tick it off.

1. Tick the **checkbox** next to a task
2. A window will appear — **write a description** of what was done
   - This is **required** — you cannot skip it
   - The client can see these notes in their portal, so keep it professional
   - Example: *"Foundation trench dug to 300mm depth and levelled. Weed membrane laid."*
3. Optionally attach **photos or files** (recommended — see Uploading Photos below)
4. Click **✅ Mark Complete**

> **Good notes example:** "Frame posts set in concrete and allowed to cure. All posts checked for vertical alignment."
> **Poor notes example:** "Done posts lol"

---

### Step 4 — Mark the Stage as Done

Once **all tasks** in a stage are ticked complete:

1. The **✓ Done** button will become active (it's greyed out until all tasks are complete)
2. Click **✓ Done**
3. A confirmation window appears — click **✓ Yes, Mark Complete**
4. The stage moves to ✅ Done

> The system automatically updates the pipeline in the office — you don't need to do anything else.

**Move to the next stage and repeat.**

---

### Undoing a Stage (If You Made a Mistake)

If you marked a stage Done by accident:
1. Click the **Undo** button on that stage — it appears straight after marking Done
2. The stage goes back to In Progress
3. You can only undo the **most recently completed** stage

> If Undo is not available, contact BCF admin — they can help correct it.

---

## Uploading Photos

Photos are evidence of your work and are visible to the client in their portal. Upload photos at each stage.

### When Completing a Task (Recommended Method)
- When you tick a task and write your notes, there is a **file upload section** at the bottom of the window
- Tap **📎 Tap to add photos or files** to choose files from your phone or computer
- Select one or multiple photos
- They will be attached when you click **✅ Mark Complete**

### Adding More Photos to a Completed Task
1. Expand the stage
2. Click **Edit** next to the completed task
3. Add new photos in the file upload section
4. Click **💾 Save Changes**

### Viewing All Photos for a Job
- In the right column, click **📂 View All by Stage & Task**
- A gallery opens showing all photos grouped by stage and task

## Client Details — Right Side Panel

The **Client Details** card shows everything you need to know about the job:

| Field | What It Shows |
|-------|--------------|
| Name | Client's full name |
| Email | Client's email address |
| Phone | Client's phone number |
| Address | The installation address |
| Product | What is being installed |
| Notes | Internal notes from the BCF office |
| **Access Notes** | Instructions the client has left for you |

### Access Notes — Read These Before You Arrive

The client may have added access instructions — things like gate codes, where to park, or whether there's a dog in the garden. Check this **before you set off**.

If the client updates their access notes while you're logged in, you'll receive a notification straight away.

---

## Birthday Bookings 🎂

If a job shows a **🎂 Birthday Booking** banner:
- This installation is for a birthday gift
- **Remember to bring freebies or a small gift** for the occasion
- Make the installation day feel special for the client

You will also receive a notification when a job is flagged as a birthday booking.

---

## Your Notifications

Tap **🔔 Alerts** (mobile) or click the bell icon (desktop) to see your notifications.

You will be notified when:

| What Happened | Notification You'll See |
|--------------|------------------------|
| You're assigned to a new job | 🔨 New Job Assigned |
| The installation date is confirmed or changed | 📅 Installation Date Set |
| The time window is updated | 🕐 Time Window Updated |
| The job is flagged as a birthday booking | 🎂 Birthday Booking! |
| The product order details change | 📦 Product Order Updated |
| The office adds internal notes | 📝 Job Notes Updated |
| The client updates access notes | 📝 Access Notes Updated |

**Marking notifications as read:**
- Tap a notification to mark it as read
- Tap **Mark all read** to clear them all

## Your Profile

Keep your profile up to date so the office and clients can identify you.

**On mobile:** Tap **👤 Profile** in the bottom nav
**On desktop:** Click your name/avatar card in the left sidebar

---

### Changing Your Profile Photo
1. Tap/click your avatar (the circle with your photo or initials)
2. Or click the **📷 Change Photo** button
3. Choose a photo from your device
4. It saves automatically — your photo now appears on the admin's order view

---

### Changing Your Display Name
1. Find the **Display Name** section
2. Edit the name field
3. Click **💾 Save Name**

---

### Changing Your Password
1. Find the **Change Password** section
2. Enter your new password (at least 8 characters)
3. Enter it again to confirm
4. Click **🔒 Update Password**
5. A green tick will confirm it was saved

## Daily Workflow — Quick Checklist

Use this every day you are on site:

**Before leaving for site:**
- [ ] Log in and open the job
- [ ] Read the **Access Notes** on the right side panel
- [ ] Check if it is a 🎂 **Birthday Booking** (bring freebies if so)
- [ ] Note the client's phone number in case you need to call

**On site — as you work:**
- [ ] Mark the first stage as **Start** when you begin
- [ ] Complete each task as you go — tick the checkbox and write notes
- [ ] Take photos at each stage and attach them to the task
- [ ] Mark the stage **Done** when all tasks in it are complete
- [ ] Move to the next stage and repeat

**Before leaving site:**
- [ ] All stages for today's work are updated
- [ ] All photos are uploaded
- [ ] Any tasks added are saved
- [ ] Confirm with client if required

## Common Problems & Fixes

| Problem | What to Do |
|---------|-----------|
| I can't see my job | Log out and log back in. If still missing, contact BCF admin to confirm you are assigned |
| The ✓ Done button is greyed out | You have incomplete tasks. Tick every task in the stage first |
| My photo didn't upload | Check your internet connection. Try a smaller photo. Make sure it's a JPG, PNG, or HEIC file |
| I marked a stage Done by accident | Click Undo immediately. You can only undo the most recent stage |
| My name or photo isn't saving | Refresh the page and try again. If still not saving, contact BCF admin |
| I forgot my password | Contact BCF admin — they can reset it for you |
| My login link isn't working | Links expire. Contact BCF admin and ask for a new temporary password |
| I'm getting an error when logging in | Check you're using the right email. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended) |

---

## Tips for a Great Record

- **Take photos at every stage** — the client sees these and it builds trust
- **Write clear task notes** — imagine explaining it to someone who wasn't there
- **Mark stages Done as soon as they're complete** — it updates the client's view immediately
- **Check access notes before every visit** — the client may have updated them
