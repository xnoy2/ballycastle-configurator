# BCF Client Portal Guide
**Ballycastle Climbing Frames — Your Online Portal**

Welcome to your personal BCF portal! This guide walks you through everything you can see and do here.

---

## Accessing Your Portal

### How to Log In
The BCF Team will provide you with a **temporary password**. Use it to log in for the first time, then set your own password when prompted.

> **If you can't log in:** Contact the BCF Team and ask them to reset your password.

---

## Your Portal at a Glance

Once inside, you will see a row of tabs at the top of the screen. Here is what each one does:

| Tab | What You'll Find There |
|-----|----------------------|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of your entire order |
| **Configurator** | Your climbing frame design |
| **Build Progress** | Live updates from your installation team |
| **Photos & Files** | All site photos from your installation |
| **Delivery** | Your installation date, time, and access details |
| **Extras & Variations** | Add-ons and change requests |
| **Refer a Friend** | Share BCF and earn rewards |
| **Reminders** | Your notifications and alerts |
| **Payments** | Your payment schedule and balance |
| **Documents** | All documents related to your order |

---

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your home screen. It shows a quick summary of your order:

- **Quote Status** — where your order is in the approval process
- **Build Progress** — how much of your installation is complete (shown as a percentage)
- **Payment Status** — how much of the total you have paid
- **Installation Date & Time** — when your climbing frame will be installed

**Birthday Booking?**
If your installation is for a birthday, you will see a special banner on your dashboard. We've noted it and will make the day extra special! 🎂

---

## Build Progress

This tab shows you exactly what stage your installation is at — updated by your worker in real time.

### What You're Looking At

Each row is a **build stage** — a major step in your installation:
- ⏳ **Pending** — not yet started
- 🔄 **In Progress** — the team is working on this
- ✅ **Done** — completed

Click on any stage to expand it and see the individual tasks. When a task is completed, your worker's description of the work appears underneath — this tells you exactly what was done.

### What to Expect
- Stages are completed in order — you won't see a later stage start until the earlier one is finished
- The **progress bar** at the top updates as each stage is completed
- You do not need to do anything here — it is updated by your worker

---

## Photos & Files

All photos taken during your installation are stored here, organised by build stage and task.

### Viewing Photos
- Photos appear in a grid, grouped by stage
- Click any photo to view it **full screen**
- Use the close button (✕) to go back

### Downloading Files
- Any documents or files attached to your build (specs, certificates, etc.) appear as download links
- Click the file to open or download it

---

## Delivery

This tab shows your installation details and is where you can add important access information for your worker.

### What You Can See
- Your full installation address
- Confirmed installation date
- Time window (e.g., "9:00 am – 12:00 pm")
- Your assigned worker's name and photo

### Adding Access Notes ← Important

If your worker needs to know anything special to access your property, add it here **before your installation day**.

**Examples of access notes:**
- Gate code: 1234
- Dog in the back garden — please ensure gate is closed
- Park on the left side of the driveway
- Key is with the neighbour at No. 12

**How to add access notes:**
1. Click the **Delivery** tab
2. Find the **Access Notes** field
3. Type your instructions
4. Click **Save**
5. Your worker is notified immediately

> **What to prepare before installation day:**
> - Clear the installation area of furniture and obstacles
> - Ensure someone is home during the time window
> - Make sure vehicle access is available if equipment needs to be unloaded nearby
> - Check your access notes are saved in the portal

## Payments

This tab shows your complete payment picture.

### What You'll See

| Section | What It Means |
|---------|--------------|
| **Contract Total** | The full agreed price for your installation |
| **Payment Schedule** | The milestones you need to pay (e.g., 50% Deposit, Final Payment) |
| **Amount Paid** | What BCF has recorded as received |
| **Balance Due** | What remains outstanding |
| **Payment Progress Bar** | Visual percentage of total paid |

### If a Payment Is Not Showing

Payments are manually recorded by BCF. If you have made a payment that is not appearing:
1. Contact BCF directly
2. Provide: date paid, method (bank transfer, card, etc.), amount, and any reference number
3. BCF will record it and your balance will update

## Extras & Variations

### Extras
Browse available add-ons for your climbing frame (e.g., extra swing, upgraded slide). If you'd like to add something to your order, select it here and BCF will be in touch.

### Variations
If you want to change something from your original order:
1. Click **Variations**
2. Describe the change you'd like
3. Submit the request
4. BCF will review it and update the status:
   - **Approved** — the change has been agreed
   - **Not Approved** — BCF will explain why in the notes

---

## Refer a Friend

Love your BCF climbing frame? Share the experience and earn a reward!

1. Click **Refer a Friend**
2. Copy your unique referral link or fill in your friend's details
3. If they place an order, you'll receive your reward automatically
4. Track the status of your referrals in this tab

---

## Notifications (Bell Icon / Reminders Tab)

The **bell icon** (top corner) shows your notifications. You'll be notified when:

- Your installation date is confirmed or changed
- A build stage is completed
- New photos are uploaded
- BCF sends you a message
- A document is uploaded to your portal

**Reading notifications:**
- Click a notification to mark it as read
- Click **Mark all read** to clear them all at once

---

## Common Questions

**Q: I can't log in.**
Contact the BCF Team and ask them to reset your password.

**Q: My build progress is showing 0% but I know work has started.**
Progress is updated by your worker. If it hasn't been updated, contact BCF — they will follow up with the team on site.

**Q: A payment I made isn't showing in my portal.**
Payments are manually logged by BCF. Contact the office with your payment details (date, method, amount, reference) and they'll update the record.

**Q: I can't see a document I was expecting.**
Documents are uploaded by BCF. If something is missing, contact the office and they'll add it.

**Q: I need to change my installation date.**
Contact BCF directly. Once they update it, you'll receive a notification in your portal automatically.

**Q: The page isn't loading properly.**
Try refreshing your browser (press F5 or pull down to refresh on mobile). If that doesn't help, try a different browser (Chrome or Safari work best).
